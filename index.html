Get Started

About League of Legends

League of Legends is a team-based strategy game where two teams of five players battle to destroy the enemy Nexus. Each player selects a champion with unique abilities and works together with their team to achieve victory. The game requires strategic thinking, quick reflexes, and effective communication.

The Five Roles

Top

The top lane is typically occupied by tanky or bruiser champions who can sustain themselves in a solo lane.

Jungle

The jungler roams the map, killing neutral monsters and assisting lanes with ganks.

Middle

The mid lane is often home to mages or assassins who can deal high damage and roam to other lanes.

ADC (Attack Damage Carry)

The ADC is a ranged damage dealer who relies on their support to stay alive and deal consistent damage in team fights.

Support

The support assists the ADC and the team by providing vision, healing, or crowd control.

Tips & Tricks

Learn the Map

Keep an eye on the minimap to track objectives and player movements.

Communicate with Your Team

Use pings and chat to coordinate strategies and alert your team to dangers.

Place Wards

Place wards in key locations to gain vision of the map and alert your team to enemy movements.

Meet Some Champions

Malphite

A durable tank who can initiate team fights and absorb damage.

Warwick

A jungler with strong sustain and the ability to hunt down low-health enemies.

Ahri

A mobile mage with strong burst damage and crowd control.

Ashe

An ADC with high damage output and the ability to snowball fights.

Lux

A mage with strong crowd control and the ability to deal damage from a distance.
